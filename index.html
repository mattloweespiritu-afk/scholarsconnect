Why ScholarsConnect?

Designed to Reduce Student Scholarship Problems

About ScholarsConnect

Built for Filipino Students. Powered by Technology.

ScholarsConnect is a web-based scholarship management portal designed for higher education institutions in the Philippines. It centralizes scholarship applications, document tracking, application review, and awardee management in one platform.

It helps reduce long queues, paper-based processing, unclear application status, and transparency gaps in scholarship selection.
